- Multi-channel play description (online + USSD + agent) — Ghanaian users expect all three.
- Multiple contact phone numbers in the footer — local convention.
- Prominent "Play responsibly. 18+" in the footer.
- Clear separation of corporate (About / T&Cs) and product (Results / How to Play) navigation.

- Visual design wholesale.
- "Buy Tickets Now" online-purchase flow (out of scope per §9 of super prompt).
- App-download CTAs (we are not shipping a mobile app in this phase).
- The vague "Transparent, Fair & Professional" tagline — we need a sharper charity-led statement.
